# Academic Researcher

**For researchers: go from scattered papers to a synthesized, citation-clean academic draft.** — built in-house by [Skill&nbsp;Me](https://skillme.dev).

For grad students, academics, and research analysts who need defensible writing, not just notes. Reach for this when you have a pile of papers and a deadline: run a formal PRISMA review or a narrative synthesis, pin down themes and gaps, verify every claim and citation, then ship a thesis-driven essay or brief that survives peer review. The full path from literature to a draft you can defend.

⭐ **If this is useful, star the repo** — it's how we gauge what to build next.

## Install

- **From the catalog:** [skillme.dev/pack/academic-researcher](https://skillme.dev/pack/academic-researcher) — install the whole pack into Claude in one step.

## Skills in this pack

- **[Systematic Review](skills/systematic-review/SKILL.md)** — Runs a PRISMA-compliant systematic review from PICO question and pre-registered protocol through search strings, two-stage screening, risk-of-bias appraisal, and GRADE-rated synthesis.
- **[Literature Review](skills/literature-review/SKILL.md)** — Produces a thematic literature review that organizes a field into an argument - consensus, disputes, evidence quality, and the open gap the reader should care about.
- **[Citation Tracker](skills/citation-tracker/SKILL.md)** — Verifies that every citation is real, unretracted, and complete, rates source quality by evidence tier, and formats reference lists precisely in APA, MLA, Chicago, or IEEE.
- **[Academic Essay](skills/academic-essay/SKILL.md)** — Structures and drafts argumentative academic essays - a contestable thesis, claim-evidence-analysis body paragraphs, a fairly-engaged counterargument, and a synthesizing conclusion - marking every spot that needs a real citation instead of inventing sources.
- **[Policy Brief](skills/policy-brief/SKILL.md)** — Writes a concise, evidence-based policy brief - problem, options compared on consistent criteria, and a recommendation - that a decision-maker can act on in minutes.
- **[Fact Checker](skills/fact-checker/SKILL.md)** — Verifies discrete factual claims against multiple independent sources and returns a calibrated verdict - Verified, Likely true, Likely false, Unverifiable, or False - with confidence and citations.
